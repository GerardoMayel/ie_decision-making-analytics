## 📈 Key Methodology Highlights

### 1. Shape Indicator Audits
Before feeding data into any predictive or pricing optimization algorithm, distributions are audited for statistical shape properties:
* **Asymmetry Evaluation (`.skew()`):** Identifying long-tailed data profiles (e.g., high-value insurance claims or extreme revenue distributions) to isolate outliers that artificially warp the sample mean.
* **Tail-Weight Detection (`.kurt()`):** Evaluating excess kurtosis to measure systemic exposure to extreme tail events (leptokurtic profiles), ensuring risk models account for low-probability, high-impact events.

### 2. Experimental Guardrails
All engineering workflows in this repository enforce baseline statistical controls to eliminate human and analytical bias:
* Enforcing strict randomness and proper sample sizing over data collections to eliminate selection bias.
* Employing robust visual controls (A/B testing architectures, standardized charts) to ensure data presents objective operational truths, completely independent of prior management hypotheses.
